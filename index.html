Hey there! My name is Vin (they/them) and I’m a Korean/American Product Designer and Illustrator with a focus on accessibility and sustainability.

I’ve always viewed art and design as a form of communication between me and the viewer. It has helped me translate my thoughts and experiences into a webpage, an illustration, or a logo just to name a few! My workflow and style are always constantly evolving as time passes and I grasp new skills and concepts. To that end, I think I’ll always see myself as a both the student and teacher with each new project.

In my free time I love sketching out or browsing fashion ideas on Pinterest or Instagram, while also watching cooking and home tour videos in the background. My ever growing interests always lead me to meeting new people and adding to my skillset as time goes on and I couldn’t be more thankful for all the wonderful people in my life!
