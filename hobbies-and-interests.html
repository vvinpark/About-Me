Hobbies and Interests

I have a variety of interests including custom keyboard construction, arcade gaming (particularly rhythm gaming), and illustration! I seem to pick up a new one every single day so enjoy seeing some of my work!

The ones that standout the most would be:

  • Custom Keyboard (Keebs) Creation
  • Rhythm Gaming (Especially at Round 1)
  • Illustration
  • Skateboarding
  • Photography

Skateboarding was something I picked up in place of longboarding. Originally I wanted to start with that but unfortunately, it was apparently much more difficult to learn, so sadly I had to resign myself to skateboarding first. A smaller board was certainly a challenge, and tons of bruises but it was well worth the work.
